# Request Account Deletion

To permanently delete your Plantastic account and all associated data, please follow the steps below:

1. Send an email to **lukas96heller@gmail.com**.
2. Please use the subject line: **"Account Deletion Request"**.
3. You **must** send the email from the email address that is associated with your Plantastic account.

## What happens when I request deletion?

Upon receiving your request, we will process it and permanently delete the following data within 30 days:
*   Your Plantastic user account and authentication credentials.
*   Your profile data (display name and profile picture).
*   Any plants, images, or watering schedules you have created within the app (if you are the owner of the household).
*   Your association with any households.

_Note: If you delete your account directly through the settings menu inside the Plantastic app, your data will be deleted immediately._
